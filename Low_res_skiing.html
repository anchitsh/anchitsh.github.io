Low Res Skiing

This game was developed for the NOKIA 3310 JAM 3 (2021), it was developed in 48 hours. The restriction for the jam is to make a game that is restricted to the visual limitations of Nokia 3310 phone.

This means the game has to limit the base resolution to 84*48, and limit to the two specific colors

Play it!

The restrictions turned out to be a blessing in disguise since I was going to work on the jam alone. Without an artist I was worried about the look of the game. But since its so low res I was able to make my basic art look actually kinda nice.

There are trees, coins, ski-lifts, fast-arrow-pickups, and a bunch more interesting things in the game!
