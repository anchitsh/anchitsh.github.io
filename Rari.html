ANCHIT SHARMA.

House Of Rari

This demo was made in one month for a company called Rari, the original pitch asked for webgl demo with a scifi environment where the users can walk around and interact with a few things and be somehow directed to their website.

I partenered with Rari's 3D artist and we also bought a few 3D Models from Unity's asset store.

So we got to work!

So I set up the character controller, the environment and the general lighting stuff. Then I added in the some cool holographic shaders on the inner capsules and the dancing model.


Next up was setting up the Vauld and making it interactable and having it animate correctly. Also some cool god rays.

I added in some basic UI and clicking on it after entering the vault would open up Rari's website in a new tab.


And finally the start screen, the password screen and the loading screen.
