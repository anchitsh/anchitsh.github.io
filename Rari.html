House Of Rari

This demo was made in one month for a Tech company (Rari), their original pitch asked for webgl demo with a scifi environment where their users can walk around and interact with a few things and be somehow directed to their website.

I partnered with Rari's 3D artist to work on this. So we got to work!

I set up the character controller, the environment and the general lighting stuff. Then I added in the some cool holographic shaders on the inner capsules and the dancing model.


Next up was setting up the vault, making it interactable and having it animate correctly. Also some cool god rays.

I added in some basic UI and clicking on it after entering the vault would open up Rari's website in a new tab.


And finally the start screen, the password screen and the loading screen.
