Super Eyebrow Princess

Year: 2021
Role: Programmer/Level Designer
Tech: Unity, C#
Platforms: PC


This game was developed for a week-long jam in November 2020.

The idea came from reversing a trope and making the princess save the knight from the dragon.

We couldn't think of a good name for it, so we named it after the titular angry eyebrowed princess.

We accomplished a lot in the one week of the jam, we finished a lot of polished levels and gameplay.
