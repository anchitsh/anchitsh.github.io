Carpe Demons

Year: 2020
Role: Programmer
Tech: Unity, C#
Platforms: PC


Carpe Demons is a Rhythm game where you fight demons by casting spells at them by drawing the correct shape on the grid. The idea came from making a game on a phone's lock screen.

Drawing the correct shapes in beat gives a higher chance of critical hits whereas casitng the speels off beat increases the chance of missing the demon.

Here is a drive link to the android prototype

Art by Ranjan Uprety





There is a demon for every minute, with boss battles at half hour and hour mark. This total comes to 1440 demons for the whole day.

On the programming side of things, the first step was working on a password pattern system, I made this with line renderers and built it on my android phone.





Then came actually recognising the required patterns, like squares, trianagles, etc. ( more to be added)

Adding some basic art, we had our first simple prototype.
