Fears to Fathom - Woodbury Getaway

I worked as a Lead Developer, on Fears to Fathom - Woodbury Getaway, the 5th game of the horror series.

It was my second time being part of the development and launch for the series, and each experience was incredible.

I worked on a really cool fishing mechanic in this game. Implementing the rope physics for it was a major challenge while also sticking to our deadlines, and still having it look good. But we got it done!

It was amazing seeing so many awesome streamers play the game, and we were thrilled that it has already gained millions of views on YouTube and Twitch.
