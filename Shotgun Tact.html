Shotgun Tact

I worked on Shotgun-tact in a 48-hour-long game jam, it is a time-slow-down topdown shooter. It won the first place at the WTF Jam 2024.

The 48-hour-long game jam happened over a month ago, there were over 1900 participants and at the end over 340 submissions.

The teams that made the top 10 games were flown to Bangalore, India for studio visits and the final winners were picked at the pitching event with a final round of judging by Nikhil Kamath (co-founder of Zerodha), Sean Hyunil Sohn (CEO of Krafton India) and Joseph Kim (CEO of Lila Games)
